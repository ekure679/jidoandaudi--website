Tailoring & Fashion Design Laboratory

Our tailoring laboratory is a modern, well-lit space equipped with professional-grade equipment that mirrors industry standards. Students gain hands-on experience with the same tools and machines they'll use in their careers.

Equipment & Features:
  • Industrial sewing machines
  • Overlock machines
  • Buttonhole machines
  • Steam pressing stations
  • Large cutting tables
  • Pattern storage systems
  • Fabric testing equipment
  • Design workstations
Safety Features:

Emergency stop buttons, proper ventilation, first aid stations, and fire safety equipment ensure a secure learning environment.

Aquaculture & Fishery Laboratory

Our aquaculture laboratory provides a controlled environment for students to learn about fish breeding, water quality management, and aquatic ecosystem maintenance using modern technology and monitoring systems.

Laboratory Equipment:
  • Water quality meters
  • pH testing equipment
  • Oxygen monitoring systems
  • Temperature controllers
  • Filtration systems
  • Breeding tanks
  • Feed testing kits
  • Microscopes
Practical Learning:

Students practice fish health monitoring, water chemistry analysis, and breeding techniques in our 12 specialized tanks and research stations.

Beauty & Cosmetology Laboratory

Our beauty laboratory features professional-grade equipment and stations that replicate a modern salon environment, providing students with realistic training experiences in hair styling, skincare, and beauty treatments.

Professional Equipment:
  • Hair styling stations
  • Professional hair dryers
  • Hair steamers
  • Facial beds
  • UV sterilizers
  • Professional mirrors
  • Tool sanitization units
  • Product storage systems
Hygiene Standards:

Strict sanitation protocols, disposable materials, and professional-grade disinfection systems ensure the highest standards of cleanliness and safety.
