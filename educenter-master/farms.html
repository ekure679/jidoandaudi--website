Aquaculture Systems & Technology

Advanced farming systems that prepare students for modern commercial aquaculture operations

Recirculating Aquaculture System (RAS)

Closed-loop water recycling system with biological, mechanical, and UV filtration for intensive fish production with minimal water usage.

System Components:
  • Drum filters for solid waste removal
  • Biofilters for ammonia conversion
  • UV sterilizers for pathogen control
  • Oxygen generation systems
  • Automated monitoring and control
Water Exchange: <95% recycling efficiency
Production: 50kg/m³ annually

Traditional Pond Systems

Extensive pond systems for understanding natural aquaculture processes, polyculture techniques, and ecosystem management.

Pond Types:
  • Nursery ponds for fry development
  • Grow-out ponds for market production
  • Broodstock ponds for breeding
  • Treatment ponds for quarantine
  • Integrated agriculture systems
Total Area: 2.5 hectares
Production: 8-12 tonnes annually

Monitoring & Control Systems

Digital aquaculture management systems providing real-time monitoring and automated control of critical parameters.

Monitoring Parameters:
  • Water temperature and pH levels
  • Dissolved oxygen concentrations
  • Ammonia and nitrite levels
  • Fish feeding behavior
  • Growth and mortality rates
Data Points: 24/7 continuous monitoring
Alerts: SMS and email notifications

Feed Management System

Automated feeding systems and feed quality management for optimal growth rates and feed conversion efficiency.

Feed Technologies:
  • Automatic feeder timers
  • Demand feeding systems
  • Feed quality testing lab
  • Custom feed formulation
  • Feed conversion tracking
FCR Target: 1.2:1 for tilapia
Feed Types: 8 different formulations
