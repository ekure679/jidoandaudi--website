Meet Our Admission Standards

General Admission Requirements

All applicants must meet these basic requirements to be considered for admission

Age Requirement

Minimum age of 16 years at the time of application. No maximum age limit for adult learners.

Educational Background

Completion of primary education (minimum) or equivalent. Secondary education preferred but not mandatory.

Language Proficiency

Basic understanding of English or willingness to undertake English language support classes.

Health & Wellness

Physical and mental fitness to participate in practical training activities safely.

Program-Specific Requirements

Each program has additional requirements tailored to its field of study

Tailoring & Fashion Design

Specific Requirements:
  • Creative aptitude or portfolio (if available)
  • Basic understanding of measurements
  • Manual dexterity for detailed work
  • Color vision (ability to distinguish colors)
  • Willingness to work with fabrics and materials
Duration:

6-12 months (Full-time) / 12-18 months (Part-time)

Fishery & Aquaculture

Specific Requirements:
  • Swimming ability (basic level required)
  • Willingness to work outdoors
  • Physical fitness for farm activities
  • Interest in marine/aquatic environment
  • Basic mathematics skills
Duration:

8-12 months (Full-time) / 12-24 months (Part-time)

Hair Dressing & Beauty

Specific Requirements:
  • Good hygiene and grooming standards
  • Interpersonal communication skills
  • Creativity and aesthetic sense
  • Standing for extended periods
  • Fine motor skills for detailed work
Duration:

6-10 months (Full-time) / 10-18 months (Part-time)

Required Documents

Please prepare the following documents for your application

Essential Documents

Identity Document

Valid passport, national ID, or birth certificate

Educational Certificates

Primary/Secondary school certificates or equivalent

Passport Photographs

2 recent passport-size photographs (white background)

Application Fee Receipt

Proof of payment for £50 application fee

Additional Documents

Work Experience Letter

Previous employment records (if applicable)

Reference Letters

Character references from employers/teachers (optional)

Medical Certificate

Basic health clearance (for specific programs)

Portfolio/Samples

Previous work samples for creative programs (optional)
