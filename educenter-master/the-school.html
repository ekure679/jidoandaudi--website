About Our Organization

Blessed Jildo and Daudi Livelihood Transformation Initiative

A registered national indigenous organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable populations through comprehensive skills development and sustainable livelihood programs.

Established: October 31, 2024
Registered: April 24, 2025
Base: Lira City West, Uganda
Category: National Indigenous NGO
Learn More View Programs

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable populations including youth, women, disabled persons, and marginalized groups by providing comprehensive skills training, improved farming practices, and sustainable livelihood opportunities for dignified living.

Our Vision

Our Organizational Objectives

Comprehensive aims designed to transform lives and strengthen communities across the Lango Sub Region

Agricultural Business Development

To encourage and support farming as a business in order to enhance food security and improve the livelihoods of communities through modern farming techniques and sustainable improvements.

Child & Vulnerable Support

To support and care for orphans and vulnerable children (OVCs) by providing linkages and referrals to relevant service providers for holistic support and development.

Skills Empowerment

To empower vulnerable individuals by equipping them with entrepreneurial and vocational skills, particularly in farming and small business development for sustainable livelihoods.

Research & Innovation

To promote and support reliable research initiatives by organizing workshops, training, and experiments; collecting and analysing data to inform effective interventions.

Health & Nutrition Systems

To strengthen community health and nutrition systems in order to build resilience and help people better withstand social and economic shocks.

Community Development

To utilize available and legal platforms to implement activities that promote community development and empowerment across all operational regions.

Financial Literacy

To equip beneficiaries with entrepreneurship and financial literacy skills to enhance small business management and sustainability in competitive markets.

Savings & Loan Associations

To promote community savings and loan associations (VSLAs) as a means of encouraging financial discipline, inclusion, and long-term sustainability.

Our Strategic Approaches

Comprehensive strategies designed to create lasting impact and sustainable livelihood transformation

Skills Development & Training

Comprehensive vocational training programs focusing on practical skills, entrepreneurship, and market-relevant competencies for sustainable employment and business creation.

Community Engagement

Active involvement of local communities in program design, implementation, and evaluation to ensure relevance, ownership, and sustainable impact across all regions.

Holistic Support System

Comprehensive support including mentorship, counseling, financial literacy, and ongoing guidance to ensure beneficiaries successfully transition to independent livelihoods.

Sustainable Development

Focus on environmentally sustainable practices, renewable resources, and long-term economic viability to create lasting positive change in communities.
