BJDLTI Training Centre - Empowering Skills, Innovation, and Sustainable Livelihoods

BJDLTI Training Centre

A comprehensive skills development and agribusiness hub serving Lira District and Lira City West, providing targeted training and startup assistance to help beneficiaries treat farming as a business and establish sustainable livelihoods.

Our Commitment

BJDLTI remains dedicated to empowering communities through skills development, innovation, and sustainable agribusiness, creating opportunities for youth, women, and farmers to thrive in today's evolving economic landscape.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Food security enhancement
  • Income generation and employment
  • Modern farming technologies
  • Youth and women empowerment
  • Environmental conservation
  • Business development and entrepreneurship
