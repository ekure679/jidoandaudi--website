Master the Art of Fashion Creation

Tailoring & Fashion Design Program

Our Tailoring & Fashion Design program is designed to develop creative and technical skills in garment construction, pattern making, and fashion design. Students learn traditional tailoring techniques combined with modern fashion design principles to become skilled artisans ready for the fashion industry.

Program Highlights

Hands-on Pattern Making
Fashion Design Principles
Industrial Sewing Machines
Business & Entrepreneurship

Learn from Our Experts

Program Details

Duration: 6-12 months
Mode: Full-time / Part-time
Entry Level: Beginner Friendly
Class Size: 15 students max
Age Requirement: 16+ years
Start Date: March, July, November

Curriculum Overview

Comprehensive training covering all aspects of tailoring and fashion design

Module 1: Foundation Skills

Duration: 6-8 weeks
  • Basic sewing techniques
  • Fabric knowledge and selection
  • Measuring and fitting
  • Thread and needle selection
  • Hand stitching techniques
  • Safety protocols in tailoring

Module 2: Pattern Making

Duration: 8-10 weeks
  • Basic pattern drafting
  • Bodice and sleeve patterns
  • Skirt and trouser patterns
  • Pattern grading and sizing
  • Pattern modifications
  • Digital pattern making basics

Module 3: Garment Construction

Duration: 10-12 weeks
  • Machine sewing techniques
  • Seam finishes and techniques
  • Buttonholes and closures
  • Professional pressing techniques
  • Garment assembly methods
  • Quality control and finishing

Module 4: Fashion Design & Business

Duration: 6-8 weeks
  • Fashion design principles
  • Color theory and coordination
  • Fashion illustration basics
  • Customer service skills
  • Pricing and costing
  • Starting your tailoring business

Career Opportunities

Diverse career paths in the fashion and tailoring industry

Independent Tailor

Start your own tailoring business offering custom clothing services to local customers

£20,000 - £40,000/year
Fashion Designer

Work with fashion houses, boutiques, or start your own fashion label creating original designs

£25,000 - £50,000/year
Alterations Specialist

Specialize in garment alterations and repairs for clothing stores and dry cleaners

£18,000 - £35,000/year
Pattern Maker

Create patterns for fashion designers and clothing manufacturers in the industry

£22,000 - £45,000/year
Fashion Consultant

Provide styling and fashion advice to individuals or work with retail fashion outlets

£20,000 - £40,000/year
Costume Designer

Design and create costumes for theater, film, television, and entertainment industry

£25,000 - £55,000/year
